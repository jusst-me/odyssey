# Product Vision

## Background

`@albanie_reizen` is an Instagram account run by the co-founder that has built a large
organic audience interested in traveling to Albania. The goal is to convert that audience
into a real business through **affiliate marketing** via **TradeTracker**.

## Mission

Build the best independent, Dutch-language travel resource for Albania — honest guides,
practical tips and curated offers — and monetize it through affiliate links and (later)
display advertising, in a way that is genuinely useful to the reader.

## Model

- **Content** (editorial guides, destinations, practical info) drives organic and social
  traffic and builds trust.
- **Affiliate offers** sourced from TradeTracker XML feeds (hotels, car rental, tours,
  insurance, etc.) are presented in context; the business earns commission on conversions.
- **Display advertising** slots are a secondary revenue stream.

## Audience

Dutch-speaking travelers researching or booking a trip to Albania — from budget backpackers
to comfort/luxury travelers and families.

## Expansion

Albania is market #1. The platform is built multi-market from day one: successful patterns
(UI, CMS models, feed integrations) are reused for additional countries as new apps in the
same monorepo, each with its own brand theme but shared foundations.

## Roles

- **Co-founder** — marketing, social media, content direction.
- **Justin** — front-end / platform engineering (this repository).

## Non-goals (for now)

- No user accounts / booking engine — we link out to advertisers.
- No custom ad server — use standard providers.
- No multi-language per app yet (Albania app is Dutch-only).
